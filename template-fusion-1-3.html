FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do US citizens need a visa for French Polynesia?+
No visa required for stays up to 90 days. Just a valid US passport and a return ticket.
What is your cancellation policy?+
Cancel 60+ days before check-in: full deposit refund. 30–59 days: 50% refund. Under 30 days: non-refundable. Contact us for special circumstances.
How far is the villa from the airport?+
Approximately 25 minutes from Fa'a'ā International Airport. Complimentary airport transfer included for direct bookings.
Is the 30% deposit refundable?+
Yes, fully refundable if you cancel 60+ days before check-in. See our cancellation policy for full details.
Can I pay in USD? What payment methods?+
All prices are in USD. We accept Visa, Mastercard, and Amex via Stripe, and PayPal. No hidden currency fees.
Is the infinity pool heated?+
No heating needed — Tahiti's water temperature is naturally 78–84°F (26–29°C) year-round. Perfect every day.
