FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do US citizens need a visa to visit French Polynesia?+
No visa required for US citizens for stays up to 90 days. Just a valid US passport and a return ticket.
What is your cancellation policy?+
Cancel 60+ days before check-in for a full refund of the deposit. 30–59 days: 50% refund. Less than 30 days: non-refundable. Contact us for special circumstances.
How far is the villa from Tahiti Airport?+
The villa is approximately 25 minutes by car from Fa'a'ā International Airport. We offer complimentary airport pickup for direct bookings.
Is the 30% deposit refundable?+
Yes, if you cancel 60+ days before your check-in date. See our full cancellation policy for details.
What currency do you accept? Can I pay in USD?+
All prices are in USD. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex via Stripe, and PayPal. No hidden currency conversion fees.
Is the infinity pool heated?+
The pool is not heated — but water temperature in Tahiti is naturally between 78–84°F (26–29°C) year-round, so no heater needed!
